Bridge Protocol

Every QTCL is mined, never minted. The bridge operates as an escrow relay: QTCL locked on the native chain backs wQTCL minted on Base. Total circulating supply across both chains always equals total mined supply.

Bridge Flow (Outbound: QTCL → wQTCL)

1. Call qtcl_bridge_lock with amount + Base destination
2. QTCL debited from sender, credited to bridge escrow
3. 3-of-5 oracles attest to the lock event
4. Relayer mints equivalent wQTCL on Base
5. Bridge request marked completed

Bridge Flow (Inbound: wQTCL → QTCL)

1. Burn wQTCL on Base contract
2. Relayer submits qtcl_bridge_burnProof
3. 3-of-5 oracles verify the burn
4. QTCL unlocked from escrow to destination
la 5. Bridge request marked completed

Fees & Limits

Fee: 0.1% (min 1 QTCL) · Range: 1–10,000 QTCL per request
TVL cap: 100,000 QTCL (Phase 1) · Cooldown: 60s per address
